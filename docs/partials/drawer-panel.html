Drawer <ui-drawer> is a standalone slide-in panel from any viewport edge. Works anywhere in the page — unlike <ui-app-drawer> which only works inside <ui-app>. Basic Usage Use show() / hide() methods or toggle the open attribute. Set placement for edge direction.
Open right drawer Open left drawer Open bottom drawer Right drawer content. Click backdrop or press Escape to close. Left drawer content. Bottom drawer content.
<ui-button onclick="document.querySelector('#my-drawer').show()">Open</ui-button> <ui-drawer id="my-drawer" label="Settings" placement="right"> <p>Drawer content here.</p> </ui-drawer>
