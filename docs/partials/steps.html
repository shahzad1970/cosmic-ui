Steps <ui-steps> renders a step wizard / stepper for multi-step flows like checkout, onboarding, or setup wizards. Basic Usage Set current (0-based) to the active step. Completed steps show a checkmark.
<ui-steps current="1" background="indigo-500"> <ui-step label="Account" description="Create your account"></ui-step> <ui-step label="Profile" description="Setup your profile"></ui-step> <ui-step label="Review" description="Confirm details"></ui-step> </ui-steps>
play-circle Playground Navigate through steps interactively.
